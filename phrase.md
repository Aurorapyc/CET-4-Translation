吃得饱：eating enough / having enough to eat

愈来愈：increasingly / more and more

近年来：in recent years

中国为世界和平做出越来越大的贡献：China is making more and more contributions to world peace.

科技在教育中起着越来越重要的作用：Technology is playing an increasing important role in education.
