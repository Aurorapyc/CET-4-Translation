议论文
类型一：现象类
类型二：观点类

In this day and age... 如今

 看到 “your opinion / agree or disagree / should / important / necessary” →用议论文模板
 看到 “write a letter / proposal / notice / survey response” → 切换到应用文模板 
看到 “causes / effects / reasons / trends” → 用“分析型”结构，慎用观点句
## 议论文模板：
Nowadays, there has been growing awareness that **[topic]** plays an important role in students’ daily life and future development.

On the one hand, **[advantage or viewpoint 1]**. Since **[reason]**, we are supposed to **[action]**. It is often the case that **[general example]**. For instance, **[specific example]**.

On the other hand, **[advantage/viewpoint 2 or counterpoint]**. Although **[concession]**, we still need to **[action]**. Not only will students **[benefit 1]**, but they will also **[benefit 2]**.

In a nutshell, **there is no better way to [achieve goal] than to [do something]**. It is time for us to **[call to action]**.

## 书信模板
Dear [ 称呼/Sir/Madam/Student Union ]
I  am Li Ming, a student at Guangzhou University[介绍自己的身份]. I am writing to [写信目的]
First/To begin with,[主体内容]
Second/In addition,[内容]
Finally/Last but not least,[内容]
Thank you for your time and consideration.
Sincerely,
Li Ming

tips:
在简单句后 + “because / since / which” 补一个原因
加一个举例句（For example, ...）
在结尾前加一句“让步”或“强调”

```tex
建议/提案：offer some suggestions for improving...
投诉：express my concern about...
申请：apply for the [position/program]
调查反馈：respond to your survey about
表达观点：share my opinion on...
```
