## 1. **“是”字结构，AS + N / Being + N**
条件：句子中有2个动词，其中一个动词为“是”
中国是文明古国，拥有悠久历史
As a civilized ancient country, China has a long-standing history.

爆竹是中国传统文化中的重要元素之一。它在中国又悠久的历史。
As one of the important elements in traditional Chinese culture, firecrackers have a long-standing history in China.

## 2. **“的”字结构（的 名词）**

## 3. **“有”字结构**
类型一：
中国有14亿人口
China has a population of 1.4 billion.

唐代诗人有许多著作
Tang poets had many masterpieces.

类型二：there be结构，句子的开头且“有”前无主语结构
在美国，有高度发达的芯片技术。
In America, there is a highly developed chip technology.

类型三：（句子，with...）
他是一名著名的主持人，有着很多粉丝。
He is  a famed host, with many fans.

## 4. **"随着"结构**
类型一：名词/名词词组 “的”
随着环保意识的增强
With the improvement of environmental awareness

类型二：As 句子 主语 动词
随着人们对中国传统文化越来越感兴趣
As people [^1]develop increasing/growing interest in traditional Chinese culture.

随着当地政府开始重视人们的生活质量
As the local government begins to pay extra/great attention to the life quality of people.

[^1]: 对....越来越

## 5. **"之一"结构 one of**
之一后的名词加复数
重庆大学是众多高等院校之一。
CQU is one of numerous colleges and universities.

山东是中国众多省份之一。
Shandong is one of many provinces in China.

国画是中国文化艺术种类之一
Traditional Chinese painting is among the artistic varieties of Chinese culture

中国传统文化中的重要元素之一。
One of/Among the important elements of traditional Chinese culture.

in improving health 在增强健康的方面

## 6. **无动词处理方法**
处理方法：加动词，有(have / be home to<主语必须是地点，国家>)
中国幅员辽阔，人口众多。
China has a vast territory and a large population.

海南雨水丰富，阳光充足，生物多样性。
Hainan has/is home to rich rainfall, ample sunshine and varied species.

这部电影粉丝很多，口碑很好。
The movie has many fans and a good reputation.

## 7. **无主语的处理方法**
加 ”人们“，上下文写一个主语，写被动
人们认为，通过这么做，可以驱散厄运，带来好运吉祥。
People believe that  bad luck can be dispersed and  good luck can be brought.

战后改变了世界格局。
The picture was changed after war.

## 8. **长名词处理方法**

农业生产活动
The production activities of agricluture.

经济文化社会
The development of economy, culture and society.
